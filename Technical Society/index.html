Welcome to

TECHNOVA

A Society that encourages students to imbibe their classroom learnings into solving real-world problems through utilization of their technical knowledge and innovations. We believe that the dynamism of scholastic life pours out of the study halls. It is credit to the scholarly focal point of the enthusiastic students that different clubs of TECHNOVA - CSE Technical Society are augmentations of their academic and career aspirations, which provides a forum for interaction among themselves and the rest of the world, however varied, it might be formal or casual, it draws out the zeal in young people and foster their authoritative abilities. The Technical Society inculcates a decent equilibrium of work, fun and leisure activities and ensures the all-round development of students.

Our Vision

The Technical Society aims to become a front-runner in preparing graduates to be efficient problem solvers, researchers, innovators and entrepreneurs, and making them competent professionals by enabling them to take up any kind of challenges in the Information Technology industry or research organizations they serve.

Our Mission

We tend to elevate the technical skillset of the students and get updated with the current trend of the industry standards, by conducting industry-institute interactions, technical sessions on different domains and propelling students to pursue their technical passion. With support from our alumni students’ network, we strive to excel in this all-time mission of the technical society.

The Technical Society provides personal growth, leadership and learning opportunities in engineering, technology, innovation, and design. The society has a goal to share our passion for technology by educating students about current development in the field of engineering and technology. In addition to connecting other students who have similar interests, students’ clubs allow students to interact with alumni and professionals in their fields of interest. It provides a place for the introduction of cohort study regarding principles and practices that are the foundation of understanding how engineering and technology supports each other as well as how they work and promotes the student community with their ability for invention, resourcefulness and ultimately innovation.

Git Hub Technical Club

The GitHub Students' Club is the biggest club present at Sharda University. It promotes and influences young minds towards contributing towards open source and learning new technologies. We, at GitHub Students' Club, aim to impart skills and knowledge to all those who are curious. and have a will to learn. We tackle topics from a wide range, including programming, development, design, and a lot more. Our aim is to create a self thriving network of students which encourages the culture of open source contribution, and promotes a sense of community within students. We promote an environment that encourages leadership, technical, and research skills, through active participation in the activities hosted by the club.

  • Consistent encouragement towards algorithmic and competitive programming.
  • Organizing events, workshops, and peer-2-peer classes, which focus on one's overall development as an engineer, and as a person.

Cyber Pirates Club

The Cyber Pirates club is set with a goal to guide individuals about Information security and cyber awareness which help them gain the necessary knowledge to arm themselves against modern-day computer exploits. The club is open to everybody at SET, Sharda University no matter what experience level or major they are. Our aim is to impart skills and knowledge to young minds to reduce the risk of cyber attacks and protection against the unauthorized exploitation of systems, networks and technologies. The Club invites enthusiastic students to study about cyber security and achieve Success. This club will help start and maintain a culture in the institute which will go a long way in helping students of current and future batches.

  • Events related to Cybersecurity & Digital Forensic

  • Peer-2-sessions
